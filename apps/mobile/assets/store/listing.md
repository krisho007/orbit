## Full Description
Stop forgetting important conversations. Start building stronger relationships.

Orbit is your personal CRM powered by an AI assistant that helps you stay on top of every relationship in your life. Whether you're a professional managing a growing network, a freelancer nurturing client relationships, or someone who simply wants to be more intentional about staying in touch -- Orbit makes it effortless.

MEET HUSKY, YOUR AI ASSISTANT
Talk to Orbit like you'd talk to a friend. Say "I had coffee with Sarah today, she mentioned she's starting a new job in March" and Husky takes care of the rest -- logging the conversation, updating Sarah's details, and suggesting a follow-up reminder. Voice input makes it even faster: just dictate and go.

CONTACT MANAGEMENT, SIMPLIFIED
Store everything that matters about the people in your life: photos, companies, job titles, notes, and custom tags. Import from Google Contacts to get started in seconds. Swipe on any contact to call or message them on WhatsApp instantly.

LOG EVERY CONVERSATION
Never wonder "what did we talk about last time?" again. Log calls, meetings, emails, and WhatsApp chats with full details -- participants, topics, follow-up actions. Multiple media types let you capture conversations however they happen.

SMART REMINDERS THAT ACTUALLY HELP
Orbit suggests reminders based on your conversations automatically. Set one-time or recurring reminders (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly) linked directly to contacts. Birthdays, check-ins, follow-ups -- nothing slips through the cracks.

EVENTS & MILESTONES
Track meetings, calls, birthdays, anniversaries, conferences, and social events. Add multiple participants to any event and see your full relationship timeline at a glance.

RELATIONSHIP MAPPING
Define how your contacts relate to each other and to you -- friends, colleagues, family, clients. Visualize your network as a relationship graph and discover connections you might have missed.

ORGANIZE WITH TAGS
Create custom colored tags to organize contacts your way. Filter and find exactly who you need, when you need them.

YOUR DATA, YOUR CONTROL
Privacy is not an afterthought. Every piece of data is isolated to your account. Export everything as JSON anytime. Consent controls let you decide exactly how AI features use your data. No ads. No data selling. Ever.

WORKS EVERYWHERE
Available on Android, iOS, and Web. Your relationships follow you across every device.

DARK MODE
Full dark and light theme support with automatic system detection. Easy on the eyes, day or night.

Orbit is built for people who believe relationships matter. Download it today and never lose touch with the people who matter most.
