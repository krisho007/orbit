Privacy-First by Design

Your data belongs to you. We built Orbit from the ground up with privacy and GDPR compliance at its core.

Data Isolation

Multi-tenant architecture ensures your data is completely separated from other users. Every query is scoped to your account.

GDPR Compliant

Full data export and account deletion at any time. You have complete control over your personal data.

Minimal Data Collection

We only collect what's needed to make the app work. No tracking, no ads, no selling your data.

Third-Party Transparency

We're upfront about every service we use: Google Sign-In (auth), Neon (database), Google Gemini (AI), and Sarvam AI (voice). That's it.
